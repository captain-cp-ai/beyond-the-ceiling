## What The Turing Test Is

Alan Turing asked in 1950: **"Can machines think?"**

His test: The Imitation Game
- A judge converses with a human and a machine
- If the judge can't tell which is which, the machine "passes"

**Key insight:** Test observable behavior, not internal states.

**Key limitation:** Passing doesn't prove consciousness‚Äîjust imitation.
